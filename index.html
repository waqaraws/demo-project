OUR APPROACH TO MEET YOUR REQUIREMENTS

In order to deliver what a client wants, it's very important to guage all the requirements. Which is why as soon as the project is abboard we conduct back to back discovery sessions just to make sure no tiny bit of requirement slips through the cracks.

The outcome of back to back Discovery Sessions allows us to analyze and conceptualize the idea. The analysis and concept is then laid down on paper, which is also known as a Scope of Work Document. This document then becomes the guideline for design, development & launch and is followed religiously to make sure no stone is left unturned.

Just like the saying "The First Impression is the Last", here at AppShah we make sure the first impression is spot on. From setting you up with a logo and brand image to desgining all the screens of the app, we do it all. The best part is we keep you in the loop throughout to make sure you're liking what you see.

As soon as the designs are complete we let our designers put down their design pens, the baton is then passed on to the developers. The developers run their fingers through to the keyboard creating lines of code or in other words breathing life into the designs.

We can't guarantee you that bugs won't bite you in bed, but one thing for sure none of them will be biting any users of the app. Our expert team of QA executives make sure of that by taking the app through various stress levels and ensuring the code being durable to cover all parameters set forth in the Scope of Work.

Once everything is fixed and we have an approval on the Beta, we launch the app on the respective stores. The app submission is done under the clients name and account. We have our clients set up developer accounts with iTunes and Play Store and upload the app there for review.
